Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

This policy describes what data Oath collects, why we collect it, and how we handle it. We believe privacy policies should be clear and direct.

Simple version: Oath collects only the data needed to enforce commitments. We don't sell your information. We don't track you for advertising. We don't use dark patterns.

Who we are

Oath is developed and operated by Algolinx Tech. You can reach us at support@algolinx-tech.in.

What data we collect

App usage data

Oath uses Android's Usage Access permission to monitor which apps you open and how long you use them. This is necessary to enforce the rules you set.

This data stays on your device. We do not upload it to external servers for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

Account information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for login and communication)
  • Device identifier (to sync your settings across sessions)
  • Subscription status (to verify access to features)

Commitment rules and logs

Oath stores the rules you define (blocked apps, time restrictions, enforcement settings). These are saved locally on your device and optionally synced to our servers if you enable cloud backup.

Accountability logs (records of apps you attempted to access) are stored locally unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Payment information

We do not collect or store payment details. All transactions are processed securely through Google Play's billing system. Google handles your payment information according to their privacy policy.

How we use your data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Enforce commitments: App usage data is monitored to apply the rules you set
  • Account management: Your email and credentials are used to authenticate your account and restore settings
  • Subscription verification: We check your subscription status to grant access to premium features
  • Support and debugging: If you contact support, we may review your account data to troubleshoot issues

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or selling to third parties.

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Service providers

We use Supabase to securely store account information and subscription data. Supabase operates under its own privacy policy and security standards. You can review their practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation. We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

Data storage and security

Account and subscription data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. App usage data and commitment logs are stored locally on your device unless you enable cloud sync.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials.

Your rights and control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we store about you
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings
  • Correction: You can update your account information directly in the app
  • Opt-out of cloud sync: You can disable cloud backup to keep all data stored locally on your device

To exercise these rights or request data deletion, contact us at support@algolinx-tech.in.

Permissions explained

Usage Access (android.permission.PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)

This permission allows Oath to monitor which apps you launch and how long you use them. It is required to enforce app blocking rules.

This data is used exclusively for commitment enforcement. We do not sell this data to advertisers or third parties.

Device Admin (optional)

If you enable advanced enforcement, Oath may request Device Admin privileges to prevent uninstallation during active commitment periods. This is optional and can be revoked at any time through Android settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as Oath evolves. If we make significant changes, we will notify users via email or in-app notification before the changes take effect.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Children's privacy

Oath is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Questions or concerns?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, contact us:

support@algolinx-tech.in

We aim to respond within 48 hours.
