Privacy Policy – Oath

Last updated: February 2026

Algolinx Tech ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Oath mobile application.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

Oath collects limited data necessary to provide its functionality, including:

  • App usage data (using Android Usage Access permission)
  • Subscription status
  • Basic account identifiers (if login is used)

We do NOT collect or store sensitive personal data such as passwords or payment details.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide app blocking functionality
  • Track subscription status
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Subscription and Payments

All payments are processed securely through Google Play. We do not store payment information on our servers.

Data Storage and Security

Some account and subscription information may be stored securely using Supabase infrastructure.

We take reasonable measures to protect user data.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Permissions

Oath uses the Usage Access permission solely to provide app blocking and productivity features.

This data is used locally and not sold or shared.
